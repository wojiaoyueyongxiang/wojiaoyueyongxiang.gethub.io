Maison Orcia

Maison Orcia asked me to create 9 key visuals for the launch of their brand.
I worked closely with Benjamin Guedj who designed the identity.

 

The brand offers 3 wine varietals: red wine, rosé, and white chardonnay.
The visuals are inspired by the taste, origin, and label design.
