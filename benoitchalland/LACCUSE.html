L'accusé

L'Accusé is a gourmet restaurant in the first arrondissement of Paris.

It was built in place of an old court of justice whose name is a reminder

of how we're all accused when we sit down to eat.

We created all the restaurant's assets including the identity, logo,

menus, business cards, clipboard, dinnerware and more.

Identity design: Benjamin Guedj

Set Design & 3D: Benoit Challand
